Virtual agents, embodied in robots, are often large software systems with
numerous modules. Making them work as one is a software architecture
challenge:

* How to put the modules together right?
* How to take advantage of ROS or your current middleware?
* How to deal with concurrent events and multi-threading?
* Which APIs to provide to your clients and app developers?
* How to implement the higher-level decision-making?

I have worked on these issues for more than a decade: on the behavior
execution pipeline of the NAO and Pepper robots at Aldebaran and SoftBank
Robotics Europe, on Semio's animation tooling, and on the skill system of
the Mirokai robots at Enchanted Tools. I love to get into these
challenges — while taking care of the legacy.
